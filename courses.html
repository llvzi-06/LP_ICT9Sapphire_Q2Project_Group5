Courses Offered

Explore our comprehensive selection of courses designed to enhance your skills and knowledge.

Casa

Our Casa program focuses on foundational learning through Montessori methods, developing independence, practical life skills, and sensorial awareness for young learners.

Primary

The Primary level emphasizes language, mathematics, and cultural studies using hands-on materials, fostering critical thinking and self-directed learning.

Intermediate

Intermediate students deepen their understanding of advanced academics, scientific inquiry, and social responsibility while developing leadership skills.

High School

Our High School curriculum integrates rigorous academics with real-world applications, preparing students for higher education and career success.

Senior High

Senior High offers specialized tracks in STEM, humanities, and business, equipping students with advanced knowledge and skills for college and professional pathways.
